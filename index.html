Download Stencils

Each stencil is available in 12" x 18" and 18" x 24", with or without the "download stencils from istand4.us" tagline. Click a design below to download.

It can be very difficult to source sign-making materials commercially without supporting large corporations that have proven themselves to be part of the problem. A great alternative is to ask your community to give you any coroplast (rigid corrugated plastic) signs and stands that they are no longer using.

If your area has a Maker Space, they may have cutting machines that you can use to make your own stencils.

In addition to making signs, consider finding a dirty wall section along a major roadway and using the stencil to pressure-wash the message into the dirt. Cleaning is not vandalism!
